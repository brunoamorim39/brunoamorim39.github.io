About Me
Hey there. My name is Bruno Amorim. I am a recent graduate from Purdue University (May 2021) with a Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering with a Minor in Business Management. Working through unique design problems allows me to get really creative with my solutions and how I implement them.
